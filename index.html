About Me

Hello! I'm Jorge Garcia, a passionate and driven computer science student at the University of Florida, set to graduate in June 2026. With a 3.7 GPA, I have a solid foundation in programming languages such as C++, Python, Assembly, TypeScript, HTML & CSS, and am proficient in technologies like Git, React, Next.JS, SFML, and Pygame.

Throughout my academic journey, I've taken courses in C++ and Python Programming, Data Structures and Algorithms, Intro to Computer Organization, Calculus 1-3, and Engineering Statistics, which have equipped me with a strong technical background.

I am also a Code2040 Fellow, actively participating in initiatives that promote diversity, equity, and inclusion within the tech industry. Through this fellowship, I've attended career-building workshops, facilitated community development, and advocated for Latinx and Black equity in the tech sector.

In addition to my academic and professional pursuits, I volunteer as a boxing instructor at the University of Florida, where I provide guidance and instruction to an average of 80 students, emphasizing safety and efficiency in training sessions.

Fluent in English, Spanish, and American Sign Language, I am always eager to learn, adapt, and contribute to meaningful projects. My character consists of quality-oriented work, strong communication skills, adaptability, and a determination to make a meaningful impact in the ever-evolving field of computer science.

Feel free to explore my projects and experiences to get a better understanding of my journey and aspirations. Let's connect and explore how we can make a positive impact together!
